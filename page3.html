Rimworld

Rimworld is one of my favorite strategy games, it is a massively customizable game in which you are free to tweak and create your own story. Set in the future where humans have colonized other planets. The game drops the player on a "Rimworld" basically a backwater planet. You are free to make your characters set their tasks, manage their food and build/defend your base. It's very extensize and simulates the wackiness and craziness of trying to survive a wild planted very well. A must play in my opinion if you are really into base buidling games and micromanagement.

Total Warhammer 1&2

The Total War Warhammer series is probably my favorite game series of all time. It gives you the power to build and control an army, The game is set in the warhammer universe which is a favorite fantasy franchise of mine. You play as a leader of one of the factions where you can build, customzie and grow your country; engaging in war, trade and many other quests. The game also has an online mode where you can attempt to best another player in a set battle. Overall, Very fun if you are into Warhammer or army strategy games in general.

Dawn of War

The Dawn of War series is another strategy frachise that I enjoy playing. Dawn of war is your typical battle strategy game set in Warhammer 40k. The gameplay although aged, is still very rewarding. Dawn of War is a real time strategy meaning you must make quick decisions on the fly, it is very much like another famous strategy game starcraft. The main reason I still enjoy this game now is the nostalgia, the rewarding strategy gameplay and I really like the Warhammer franchise.
