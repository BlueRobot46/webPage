Halo Series

The Halo series is one of the most iconic shooting games of all time in my opinion. My first experience with Halo was on the original Xbox, Halo has certainly been around for a long time; with each iteration building on top of each other. Halo is known for it's storyline based in a sci-fi universe where humanity is in a battle of survival against conquering aliens. Halo generally always boasts a solid single player campain where you can play co-op with friends, Multiplayer to battle online and even a forge mode where you can create your own maps and gamemodes.

Call of Duty

Call of duty is also one of my favorite action game series. I like the fact that Call of Duty is relatively easy to get into, the controls and actually learning how to play is very easy. My favorite things to do in the game is to play the multiplayer which always has a lot of players online; so the wait times to play are generally low. Online matches in Call of Duty are fairly quick making it a perfect game to play for 15-30mins at a time. Another thing I like about Call of Duty is the Zombies game mode, I like having to survive and battle it out against waves of zombies; I generally play this mode with my friends and it is a lot of fun.

Dark Souls Franchise

The Dark Souls Franchise, is another favorite of mine in the action games genere. Dark Souls is an atmospheric action survival game, the point of the game is to be difficult and punish you for playing badly. As bad as that might sound Dark Souls is actually a very rewarding game, albeit with a steep learning curve. The game is set in a grimdark medieval setting, where you need to battle monsters and bosses to complete the game; it's very linear in that sense. The main enjoyment I get out of Dark Souls is beating the bosses, beating bosses is a much more amazing experience in Dark Souls as you really need to learn and work to win. Overall, Dark Souls is an amazing game for anybody looking for a challenge.
