Skyrim

Skyrim is probably my favorite game of all time period, even when comparing other genres. Skyrim is set in a medieval fantasy setting, you are free to create your character however you want and are pretty much free to roam around and play how you want as well. You could be a warrior, thief or even magician. The game lets you buy your own property, cook and explore however you want. Skyrim also has a lot of mods from other players to give you the option to truly customize the game to your liking.

Fallout

Fallout plays very similarly to Skyrim as they are published by the same company. The main difference is that Fallout is set in a post nuclear/apocalyptic setting. Like Skyrim you are able to play how you would like, you are free to customize your character and either play as a good guy helping others or as a villian. Fallout also boasts a large selection of mods as well letting you do things like build settlements, armor and many other things which might enhance your experience. Overall, a very fun game where you can get lost in for hours.

Mass Effect

Mass Effect is another RPG that I really enjoy, unlike the last two games Mass Effect is more story based. Although you do have the option to customize your play style it is set within the context of a story. You play a commander shephard a millitary commander for the future united humanity. You will explore the stars finding allies and battling enemies in a mission to become the hero and save the universe.
